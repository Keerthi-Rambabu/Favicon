Adding Favicon

  1. Favicon stands for "favorite icon".
  2. It's a small image or icon associated with a website.
  3. Appears in the browser's address bar, bookmarks, and browser tabs.
  4. Helps users identify and differentiate between multiple open tabs or bookmarks.
  5. Typically square in shape.
  6. Commonly created in .ico format but can also be .png or other image formats.
  7. Contributes to the branding and recognition of a website.
  8. Enhances overall user experience by providing visual representation
